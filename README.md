# Repressions in Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine, 1937–1938

Data visualizations based on statistical data from:

Melnyk, O.O. (2017). Repressions of 1937–1938 in Kryvyi Rih. 
In Proceedings of the Third Historical and Local History Conference 
"Kryvorizhzhia: A Look into the Past...". Kryvyi Rih. 55-61.

## What this is about

The article documents 1,878 cases of political repression during the Great Terror in Kryvyi Rih. This repository contains:

- `data/` — CSV files extracted from the article's statistical tables
- `code/` — script for visualizing the data
- `output/` — exported html

## Preview

[View chart](https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/Olena-Tykhostup/kryvyi-rih-repressions-1937-38/blob/main/output/plot_charges.html)

## Key findings

- 73.9% of documented victims were executed
- 31% were accused of espionage

## Notes on the data

The author estimates the real total is at least 20% higher than the documented 1,878 cases, due to archive losses during WWII.
